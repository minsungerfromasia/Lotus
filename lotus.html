Welcome to Lotus and Pagoda Bites

At Lotus and Pagoda Bites, we believe that great food can create meaningful change. Our mission extends beyond serving delicious Thai and Burmese cuisine; we are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of children in rural Myanmar and Thailand.

With every meal you enjoy, you help support our initiative to improve educational opportunities for underprivileged children in remote areas. A portion of our proceeds goes directly to funding school supplies, infrastructure improvements, and educational programs.

By dining with us, you’re not just savoring authentic flavors from across Southeast Asia—you’re contributing to a brighter future for children who deserve a chance to learn and grow. Join us in our commitment to bettering lives, one bite at a time.

Taste the difference. Make a difference.
